To use the sign tool you need to install Windows SDK.

https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/downloads/sdk-archive/

Choose your version of windows. 

Once installed find it on your system. Send the Path to signtool.exe to the admininstrator of PyCrypt.

It should look something like this...C:\\Program Files (x86)\\Windows Kits\\10\\App Certification Kit\\signtool.exe
